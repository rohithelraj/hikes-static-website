Berliner Höhenweg

Map Footprint

Dates

17-09-2025 - 22-09-2025

Description

This year, we do the Berliner Höhenweg, at least the second part of it, from Berlinerhütte to Karl von Edelhütte.


Day-1 (17 September 2025)


We drive from Düsseldorf to Mayrhofen.
We start at 13:00 from Düsseldorf by car and drive to our Accomodation in Mayrhofen,
it is a 8 Hours 32 Minutes drive.

Day-2 (18 September 2025)

03:28  10,2 km  2,9 km/h  830 m ↑


We drive from  Mayrhofen, to the parking place.
We start our hike from Parkingplace in Zillertal, where we reach by Uber.

Then we start hiking from there to Berliner Hütte.
